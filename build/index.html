Hi, I’m Björn Richter,

Full Stack Designer and Frontend Engineer based in Berlin.

Currently I’m working at Solarisbank AG, a German fintech providing banking as a service. As a designer and frontend engineer I’m working on the design and implementation of the Solarisbank backoffice – the internal key tool for all kind of banking operations.
In the past I gained experience in a lot of short and long running projects for bigger and smaller clients at bitspire GmbH, Edenspiekermann AG and several other companies. I have a Bachelor’s degree in Interface Design from the University of Applied Sciences Potsdam and a degree in Media Design (IHK).
